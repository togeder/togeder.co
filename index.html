Frequently Asked Questions

What is Togeder?

Togeder is an innovative platform that combines artificial intelligence for smart Zoom room management. We enable instructors and group facilitators to manage meetings more efficiently while receiving real-time insights about room dynamics.

How does it work with existing video platforms?

Our system integrates seamlessly with Zoom and other video platforms. No special installations required - simply connect through your browser and start using it.

What insights does the AI provide?

Our AI analyzes engagement levels, interaction quality, and communication patterns in each room in real-time. The system provides recommendations for improving group dynamics and identifies situations requiring intervention.

What about security and privacy?

We maintain high standards of data security and privacy. All information is encrypted, and we comply with strict security standards. Users have control over their data and can choose what information to share.
